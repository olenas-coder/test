Recommendations
Action Items & Month 2 Strategy
Prioritized recommendations based on first-month performance data. Actions are split between client-side requirements and Impactable-managed optimizations.
⚠️ Priority — Conversion Tracking
Implement Conversions API (CAPI) for Enhanced Tracking Accuracy
A form-submission conversion event is already configured. However, with 112 landing page clicks in Month 1 and zero recorded conversions, browser-based tracking via the LinkedIn Insight Tag may be missing conversion signals due to ad blockers, iOS privacy restrictions, or cookie limitations. Implementing CAPI (server-side conversion tracking) as a parallel signal will ensure form submissions are captured regardless of browser environment, improve match rates, and give us the data needed to optimize bids and budgets toward actual pipeline in Month 2.
Client Action Required
⚠️ Priority — Funnel Expansion
Middle-of-Funnel Retargeting — Ready to Launch in Month 2
The warm audience pool has grown to nearly 500 matched members across website visitors, company page visitors, and ad engagers — surpassing LinkedIn's 300-member minimum required to activate a retargeting audience. A dedicated MOF retargeting campaign targeting warm contacts who have already engaged with CG Creative's ads or visited the website is now ready to launch. Retargeting warm audiences typically delivers 3–5× higher CTR and significantly lower CPC compared to cold TOF campaigns, making this the highest-leverage addition to the account at this stage.
Planned — Month 2
🟡 Monitor — Frequency
Watch Frequency on Sponsored Content Audiences
Average frequency on sponsored content audiences has reached 4.14× (Marketing JT) and 5.27× (Event JT) — crossing the threshold where LinkedIn best practices suggest diminishing returns and potential audience fatigue begin to set in. We recommend the following actions to maintain delivery efficiency:

1. Set a Frequency Cap — Apply a frequency cap to limit how often the same profiles are served ads within a given window, protecting reach quality and reducing wasted impressions on already-saturated profiles.

2. Test Ad Personalization — Since ad personalization is available in the account, we suggest duplicating the current campaigns and testing dynamic personalization (e.g. %COMPANYNAME%) in the introductory text of the duplicated ad versions. Personalized ads tend to stand out in feed even for profiles that have already seen the standard creatives multiple times, which can help offset frequency fatigue while the audience pool continues to grow.
Monitoring
🟢 New — Ad Format
Spotlight Ads as an Alternative to Text Ads for Website Visits
Spotlight Ads are a dynamic ad format personalized with the viewer's own LinkedIn profile data — including their name, photo, and company — creating a tailored experience that typically drives stronger engagement than standard text ads. Like text ads, they appear in the right-rail placement on desktop, but their personalization gives them a more attention-grabbing presence.

Given that CG Creative's text ads under the Website Visits objective generated $0 in spend, Spotlight Ads represent a stronger alternative for this placement. Since they run on CPC bidding and are personalized by default, they are more likely to deliver impressions and engagement where text ads did not.
